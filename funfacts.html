Fun Facts

Quick Facts

Basketball was invented in 1891 by James Naismith.
NBA game lasts 48 minutes (12 min quarters).
Basketball hoop is 10 feet high worldwide.
Averages 90-100 shots per game.

Main Facts

The first basketball had a soccer ball bottom sewn to peach basket.
NBA 3-point line is 23.75 ft (corner 22 ft).
Michael Jordan won 6 championships.
LeBron James has most playoff points ever.
Basketball in Olympics since 1936.
